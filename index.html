Our Story

Tracy & Ralph

Welcome to our little corner of horological history. We are private collectors who turned a lifelong passion for fine watchmaking into a curated selection of rare and hard-to-find ephemera: vintage brochures, instruction manuals, catalogues, collector pieces and rare documentation from the world's most prestigious brands.

Every item we list is genuine, carefully sourced, and described with honesty. No bulk lots, no fakes, no shortcuts.

We are just getting started. In 2027 we will be expanding into vintage and modern watch parts, accessories, and beyond. If you are a collector who appreciates the stories behind the watches as much as the watches themselves, you are in the right place.

Questions? Always feel free to reach out. Tell us what you are looking for, we might just have it.
